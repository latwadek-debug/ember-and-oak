Our Story

A Passion Rooted in Tradition

Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief — that dining is not merely eating, but an experience that engages every sense. Founded in 2019 by Chef Arjun Mehta, our restaurant brings together the warmth of traditional Indian culinary wisdom with the precision of modern gastronomy.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen carries a story — of hand-selected ingredients from local farms, of techniques perfected over generations, and of a relentless pursuit of excellence that defines everything we do.
